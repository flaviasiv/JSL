Move-In / Move-Out
Cleaning

Whether you're moving into a new home or preparing to hand over the keys, our move-in/move-out service ensures the space is thoroughly cleaned and ready. This detailed cleaning covers all rooms, including inside cabinets, drawers, closets, appliances, and baseboards. We remove dust, stains, and buildup from every corner, so whether you’re arriving or leaving, the property feels fresh, clean, and move-in ready. This service is ideal for homeowners, renters, landlords, and real estate professionals looking to present the space in its best condition.

Checklist

All Rooms

  • Dust and wipe down all surfaces
  • Clean baseboards, doors, and door frames
  • Clean interior windows and window sills
  • Vacuum and mop floors
  • Remove cobwebs

Checklist

Kitchen

  • Clean inside and outside of cabinets and drawers
  • Clean inside and outside of all appliances
  • Sanitize sink and faucet
  • Clean countertops and backsplash

Checklist

Bathrooms

  • Scrub and disinfect toilet, shower, tub, and sink
  • Clean inside vanities and drawers
  • Polish mirrors and fixtures
  • Clean walls and floor tiles

Checklist

Bedrooms /
Closets

  • Clean inside closets
  • Dust shelves and baseboards
  • Clean doors and handles

Checklist

Garage /
Laundry (if included)

  • Sweep floors
  • Clean shelving and cabinets
  • Wipe washer/dryer exteriors
